A short biograpy

I was born on a cold December night in Philadelphia. The day after I was born the city experienced more rain than had fallen on any single day in the previous decade. I grew up in a rowhouse in South Philadelphia, raised by my parents who were teachers and in the company of my older brother. When I was in fourth grade my parents applied for me to go to a special middle school which was called Masterman. When I was accepted many adults told me that I was smart. I attended Masterman middle school continued through High School and ended up graduating with honors. In the Fall of 2015, I matriculated into Tufts University in Medford, MA, where I currently reside. At Tufts, I study Computer Science and Education and hope to be a teacher one day.
